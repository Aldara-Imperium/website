Aldara Imperium

Aldara is a young multinational Empire ruled by a Constitutional Monarchy and is mostly made up of soldiers.
Aldara was originally formed and overseed many times by Jellal Ezra. Ever since it's creation Aldara have been both a strong and chaotic nation due to the various civil wars that used to take place within. Now the Empire have cleansed itself of the various internal threats and started to thrive and shine with newer developments and reforms.
Before being taken over by the Imperial House of Vanora, the Aldara had ben led by various houses such as Ezra, Llaman and Honoratus. Now it's Monarchy only belongs to the Ruling House of Vanora.


Aldaran Economy

Aldara's economy uses Hanors as currency and has very little tax rates. Aldarans' main form of income is working in various branches of the Empire. These are Imperial Aldaran Army, Imperial Aldaran Government and various civilian organizations.
By Aldara's new economic reforms, Aldara citizens can offer paid services to citizens or open companies and businesses within the Empire.
- Aldaran Central Bank
All citizens of Aldara pay taxes to the Aldaran Central Bank which makes loans and funding of various Empire organizations possible.
- Currency
Aldaran currency, aside from it's purposes within the Empire, can be traded for Gralats. However this feature is limited for the time being.
- Civil Organizations
Aldara's civil organizations work on various subjects to assist the Empire.


Aldaran Government

Aldara's Government is run by a Prime Minister and a council of Ministers under it as well as a National Assembly of 5 Representatives.
Prime Minister of Aldara and 5 Representatives of the Aldaran National Assembly is elected by a vote of the Aldara citizens.
- Prime Minister
Prime Minister of Aldara assigns many ministers to respective ministries and ensure their work.
- Ministers
Ministers of the Aldaran Government constantly work and produce new projects for their respective ministries to ensure the stability of the Empire.
Aldaran Ministries are:
-Ministry of Economy
-Ministry of Internal Affairs
-Ministry of Foreign Affairs
-Ministry of Defence
-Ministry of Culture
And some other ministries.
- 5 Representatives of the Aldaran National Assembly
Aldaran National Assembly is where the most critical law changes and internal/foreign actions are presented to the vote of the 5 Representatives. Representatives of the Aldaran National Assembly makes sure that no wrong change is made within Aldara.
